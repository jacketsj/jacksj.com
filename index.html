Jack S-J

Researcher and Software Engineer

jacksj at uwaterloo.ca

I am a researcher and software engineer primarily specializing in computational geometry and related fields. I am often interested in efficient, practical algorithms and approaches for problems that also come with theoretical guarantees. I also enjoy exploring lower bounds/computational complexity, constraint solving, and various forms of parallelization and hardware acceleration.

I have worked on industry production systems for high-dimensional approximate nearest-neighbour problems, FPGA middleware, and low-level distributed robotics graph-compute systems.
